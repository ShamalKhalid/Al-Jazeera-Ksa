Welcome To AL JAZEERA Service Contracting Company.

Welcome to Aljazeera, your premier service contracting company specializing in manpower supply. We deliver skilled personnel to meet your business needs efficiently.

Explore Projects

Industrial construction & support services

Al Jazeera serves clients in the petrochemical, manufacturing, imports, and exports industries. Our focus is on new facility construction, electrical instrumentation, addressable security, and other utility systems.

Civil construction and support services

Al Jazeera excels in diverse construction projects, from new builds to renovations. As a general contractor, we cover everything from roads and drainage to interior design and telecommunications.

Mechanical construction & support services

Al Jazeera specializes in electromechanical works for industrial and district cooling plants, mechanical piping, and structural projects. Our expertise extends to renovations and upgrades for existing facilities.

Equipment rental services

We provide a wide range of equipment rental services including pickups, trailers, service buses, excavators, welding machines, cranes, forklifts, and more.

Industrial support services

We provide skilled and unskilled manpower solutions tailored to long-term, short-term, and emergency requirements. Partnering with industry giants like SABIC and Saudi Aramco, we employ cutting-edge technology and recruitment strategies to deliver top talent, ensuring operational excellence for our valued clients.

Electrical construction services

Al Jazeera excels in electromechanical works for industrial and district cooling plants, mechanical piping, and structural projects, including renovations and upgrades for existing facilities.

Al Jazeera Contracting Services

Established in 2010, Al Jazeera is a distinguished joint venture with a primary focus on diverse sectors such as IT Infrastructure, Data Centers, Fire Alarm Systems, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing), Hardware and Software Solutions, Security Systems, Data Center Relocation, Communication Networks, and Human Resource Support Services. Our expertise extends to Industrial, Civil, and Mechanical Construction, as well as Electrical Installation and General Maintenance. Additionally, we offer Equipment Rentals and provide skilled professionals across various industries.
